Privacy Policy

City of Forest – Water Operations App

Last updated: June 26, 2026


Overview

The City of Forest Water Operations app ("the App") is operated by the City of Forest Public Works Department. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights with respect to that information.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

The App requires users to sign in with a username and password assigned by the City of Forest IT Department. Credentials are stored locally on your device using the browser's built-in localStorage and are never transmitted to third-party services.

Operational Data

The App stores work order entries, meter readings, maintenance logs, and related operational records that you enter. This data is saved locally on your device and, if a server sync URL has been configured by your administrator, may also be synced to your organization's internal server.

Device & Usage Information

The App does not collect analytics, crash reports, advertising identifiers, or any usage telemetry. No data is shared with Apple or any third-party analytics provider.

How We Use Your Information

Information stored by the App is used solely to:

  • Display your work orders, meter reads, maintenance tasks, and valve/hydrant records.
  • Allow authorized personnel to create, update, and complete operational records.
  • Synchronize data with the City of Forest internal server when configured by an administrator.

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any third party.

Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on the device using standard iOS WebKit localStorage. If server sync is enabled, data is transmitted over HTTPS to the City of Forest internal server only. The App does not communicate with any external cloud service.

Children's Privacy

This App is intended for use by City of Forest employees only. It is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
