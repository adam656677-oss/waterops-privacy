## Overview

Water Ops is a free, public mobile application designed to serve both the general community and public works professionals. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and how it is protected. By using this app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## Who We Are

Water Ops is developed and maintained by **Adam Taylor**, an independent developer. All data collected through this application is stored on servers owned and operated by Adam Taylor. No data is shared with or sold to third parties.

---

## Information We Collect

### Public Users (No Login Required)
Users who access the public-facing features of the app — including the Kids Zone, water facts, and educational content — do **not** need to create an account. We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from public users unless they voluntarily submit a question or contact form.

If a public user submits a question, we may collect:
- The text of the message submitted
- An optional name or email address if provided

### Operational Users (Login Required)
Users who log in with a role-based account (Water Tech, Streets, W&S Supervisor, Manager, Inventory) may have the following data stored on our server:
- Username and role assignment
- Work orders, system readings, and field notes entered within the app
- Inventory records and operational logs

This data is entered voluntarily by authorized utility staff in the course of their job duties.

---

## Children's Privacy (Kids Zone)

The Kids Zone section of Water Ops is designed for educational use by children. We take children's privacy seriously and comply with the **Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)**.

- The Kids Zone does **not** require account creation or login.
- We do **not** knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.
- The Kids Zone does **not** contain advertisements, in-app purchases, or links to external websites.
- All content in the Kids Zone (water facts, quizzes, coloring activities, and the Water's Amazing Journey game) is fully self-contained within the app.

If you believe a child under 13 has submitted personal information through this app, please contact us immediately at **adam656677@yahoo.com** so we may delete it promptly.

## How We Use Your Information

Information collected through Water Ops is used solely to:
- Provide and improve the features of the application
- Allow authorized utility staff to perform their job functions
- Respond to questions or support requests submitted through the app
- Maintain and operate the water operations platform

We do **not** use your data for advertising, marketing, or any commercial purpose.

All data is stored on servers owned and controlled by Adam Taylor. We take reasonable technical and organizational precautions to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss.

- Operational data is accessible only to authorized users with valid credentials.
- Server access is restricted and maintained by the developer.
- No data is transmitted to third-party analytics platforms, advertising networks, or cloud services outside of the developer's own infrastructure.
